Constellation

A constellation is an area in space that has a group of visible stars which forms an outline or pattern. The way they are perceived and represented varies from one constellation to another. They can be anything from an animal to a mythological creature, person or an inanimate object. We have gathered together all of our favourite constellations and written detailed guides on each of them. Take a look for yourself to find out more interesting facts and information about Constellations..
